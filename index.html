About Me

I graduated from Indiana State with a Bachelors in Information System. I started my career as a COBOL programmer in manufacturing and then healthcare. Upon graduating from Ball State with an MBA, I began working for a physician medical practice. As Director of Development, I built a company website using HTML, an enterprise RDMS using Access and Visual Basic. Within a short time, I was named Administrator and ran the practice. Through our strategic planning process, we decided to partner with a larger group. I sold the practice and began working for the acquiring corporation as Regional Client Administrator. After several years managing the practice, I was promoted to VP Practice Development. I served for 8 years as VP and achieved 114% of assigned gross profit goals. I left the practice for family time and taught computer programming for two years while maintaining a consulting company.
